Workflow Status Matrix

The idea behind this matrix is how to provide a simple display of your team, their projects, their projects' status, and any other information that could be relavent. Using this idea/tool can make planning your teams projects incredibly easier given the jQuery .sortable() functionality. I haven't decided on how to load and save the data (prehaps json) or how to create new items, but ideally, the matrix could be a simple site that users would navigate to, perhaps use multi-touch capabilities to allow easy and interactive manipulation.
